## Exercise Do's & Don'ts

### ✅ DO's

| Area | Action |
|---|---|
| **Consistency** | 5 out of 7 days minimum — missing one day is fine, missing two is a habit problem |
| **Office habits** | Stand up and walk 5 min every 1–1.5 hrs at the desk; always take stairs |
| **Morning** | Even 10 min of stretching on busy days counts — never do zero |
| **Sleep** | 7–8 hours; muscles recover during sleep — poor sleep undermines all training |
| **Hydration** | Drink 300–400 ml water before and after each workout session |
| **Breathing** | Exhale on exertion (e.g., exhale while pushing up in a push-up) |
| **Rest** | 30–60 sec rest between sets is sufficient for fat loss |

### ❌ DON'TS

| Avoid | Reason |
|---|---|
| Skipping warm-up | Increases injury risk, especially for cold muscles after desk work |
| Training through sharp pain | Distinguish between muscle burn (OK) and joint/sharp pain (stop) |
| Holding breath during lifts | Causes blood pressure spike — dangerous given elevated lipids |
| Doing only cardio, skipping strength | Strength training is essential for insulin sensitivity and muscle preservation |
| Overtraining — more is not better | Recovery is when fat loss happens; rest days are not wasted days |
| Working out right after a full meal | Wait at least 60–90 min post-meal before exercising |
