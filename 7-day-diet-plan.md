| Area | Action |
|---|---|
| **Morning** | Drink 2 glasses of warm water immediately on waking, before coffee or tea |
| **Meal timing** | Eat dinner before 8:00 PM; no food 2–3 hrs before sleep |
| **Protein first** | At every meal, eat protein first (eggs, dal, chicken) before carbs |
| **Mindful eating** | Eat slowly; avoid screens during meals; stop at 80% full |

### ❌ Dietary DON'TS

| Avoid | Reason |
|---|---|
| Maida products (noodles, manchurian, white bread, biscuits) | Personal bloating trigger; spikes blood sugar; worsens HbA1c |

| Packaged chips, namkeen, biscuits | Trans fats, excess sodium — worsens lipid profile |
| Fruit juices and soft drinks | High glycemic; raises triglycerides |
| Skipping breakfast or lunch | Leads to overeating; slows metabolism |
| Eating at the desk during work | Promotes mindless overeating |
| Late-night snacking (after 9 PM) | Impairs overnight fat metabolism |
| Alcohol | Raises triglycerides; stresses liver (ALT already near upper limit) |
| Fried restaurant food | Trans fats, excessive calories, unknown oil quality |
| Crash dieting or skipping meals | Causes muscle loss and hormonal disruption |
